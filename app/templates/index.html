How to use this site

1. Find the bid — browse or search active opportunities filtered by county, agency, or type.

2. Verify the source — every record links directly to the official agency posting. Always confirm the live notice before committing estimating time.

3. Focus on the gold documents — bid ranges, work item lists, addenda, and submission instructions are what determine whether a job is worth chasing.

Documents worth your time

Official bid notice — confirms due dates, submission method, and contacts.

Bid ranges & estimates — tells you if the job is in your lane before you spend time pricing.

Work item lists — reveals scope, subs needed, equipment, and teaming opportunities.
