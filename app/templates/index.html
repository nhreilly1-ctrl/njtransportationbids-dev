How to use this site

1. Find the bid - pick a category from the nav, then filter by county, agency, or keyword.

2. Verify the source - every record links to the official agency posting. Always confirm before committing estimating time.

3. Focus on the gold documents - bid ranges, work item lists, addenda, and submission instructions determine whether a job is worth chasing.

Documents worth your time

Official bid notice - confirms due dates, submission method, and contact information.

Bid ranges & estimates - tells you if the job is in your lane before you spend time pricing.

Work item lists - reveals scope, subs needed, equipment, and teaming opportunities.
